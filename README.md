## 3. Preceding Question Prompting:

Prompt:	"Why is climate change a global concern? Explain how greenhouse gases contribute to global warming."<br>
Output:<br>
Climate change is a global concern because it affects all countries through rising temperatures, extreme weather events, sea-level rise, food insecurity, and threats to human health and ecosystems, making international cooperation essential to address its impacts.<br>
Greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide, methane, and nitrous oxide contribute to global warming by trapping heat in the Earth’s atmosphere. These gases allow sunlight to enter but prevent some of the outgoing heat from escaping into space, creating a greenhouse effect that increases the Earth’s average temperature over time.<br>
_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
Prompt:"How do vaccines work? Describe the process of immunization in simple terms."<br>
Output:<br>
Vaccines work by training the body’s immune system to recognize and fight a specific disease without causing the illness.<br>
In simple terms, a vaccine contains a weakened, killed, or harmless part of a germ. When it enters the body, the immune system learns to identify it and produces antibodies. If the real germ later infects the body, the immune system remembers it and can quickly destroy it, preventing the person from getting sick or making the illness much milder.<br>
